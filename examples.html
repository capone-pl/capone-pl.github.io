Capone: some examples

The following are some example language product lines built with Capone:

  • A DSL family for networking together with a simulator (which gets adapted to each language variant)
  • A family of Petri nets and their simulators
  • A family of State machines and their simulators
  • A family of graph types and a family of simulators for breadth-first traversal
  • A family of automata languages
Measurements of size reduction of these examples w.r.t. an enumerative approach can be found here. Measurements of the (lifted) instantiability analysis of these examples w.r.t. an enumerative approach can be found here.
